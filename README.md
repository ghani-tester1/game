# Algorithmic Trading Tutorial

Welcome to the Algorithmic Trading Tutorial!

This tutorial is designed to provide a comprehensive introduction to the world of algorithmic trading. Whether you are a beginner with no prior experience or a seasoned trader looking to automate your strategies, this guide will walk you through the essential concepts, tools, and techniques.

## What You'll Learn

- The fundamentals of algorithmic trading
- How to set up a development environment
- Common trading strategies and how to implement them
- Backtesting and risk management
- Resources for further learning

## Tutorial Structure

The tutorial is divided into several sections, each building upon the last. You can follow the lessons in order or jump to the topics that interest you most.

- **Introduction to Algorithmic Trading**: `algorithmic_trading_tutorial.md`
- **Glossary of Terms**: `glossary.md`
